Feedback control

Publishing is no longer a monologue. When you get readers attention you better be in the mood for discussion. Editorial’s integrated tools make it easy for you to track and cultivate these conversations in form of comments, blog posts, tweets, likes etc.

Your feedback is the best test

Your feedback is the best way to test this feature. Like or tweet about our friends’ projects, blog about our theme or simply leave us a comment with your thoughts and suggestions.
