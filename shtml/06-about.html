About us

There’s an old saying in Slovenia that says if you put three talented & dedicated people in the same room you will come out with a kickass Wordpress theme and a whole bunch of creative fairy dust.

We take an interdisciplinary approach — blending the practices of design, technology and branding — to create modern interactive systems. We care about the web and its future. It has become a large part of our lives – entering our offices, living rooms and even our pockets. It is changing the way our world operates.
