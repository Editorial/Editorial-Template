Overview

Wordpress theme a la responsive

Designed and built for a wide range of devices.

Deliver your content to a broader audience than you can imagine

Unite and conquer

Days when only desktop computers and laptops would access the web are long gone. Today web lives on variety of devices. While some of them are more common than the others, your readers are equally important and they deserve a good experience regardless of their setup. Supporting desktop computers, laptops, netbooks, tablets, smart phones and everything in between is just the beginning of Editorial.

One Theme to Rule Them All

There’s so much more to it than the clever technology under the hood. Take your time to experience the comfort of reading on even the tiniest screens and appreciate the level of details that went into bringing images to life under your fingertips. Don’t just give your readers a reasons to enjoy every article you write, but allow them to effortlessly browse, share and engage with your content.

Supported devices

Even though in theory it should work on every device running a HTML capable browser, we proof tested it and guarantee flawless experience on the minimum of following devices:

  • iOS (iPhone, iPad, iPod touch)
  • Andorid ()
  • Amazon Kindle
  • Windows Phone
  • BlackBerry
  • Symbian 2.0, 3.0
  • Maemo
  • MeeGo
